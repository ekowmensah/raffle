╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

✓ Station-wide campaigns are ALWAYS visible (no programme needed)
✓ Programme-specific campaigns ONLY show when you select that programme
✓ When you select a programme, you see BOTH types of campaigns
✓ Station-wide campaigns appear FIRST in the list

Use STATION-WIDE for:
  • General promotions
  • Holiday campaigns
  • Station anniversaries
  • Cross-programme events
  • Maximum reach campaigns

Use PROGRAMME-SPECIFIC for:
  • Programme anniversaries
  • Host-specific promotions
  • Niche audience targeting
  • Exclusive listener rewards
  • Programme loyalty campaigns
